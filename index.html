Angelica Martinez Ochoa

Lecturer & Critical Media Studies Researcher

Welcome to my professional portfolio. I am a lecturer and researcher specializing in the intersection of **Arts, Technology, and Emerging Communication**. My work employs a critical media studies lens to analyze **Technocapitalism and Postpartum Labor**, exploring how digital platforms and emerging technologies shape contemporary motherhood. I am dedicated to critical analysis of digital labor, AI ethics, and social practice in media.
